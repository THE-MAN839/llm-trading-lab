Day 1: System Launch (2026-03-12)

March 12, 2026

Launched the autonomous trading research system inspired by Karpathy's autoresearch project.

What We Built

  • 3 Research Agents — Each with isolated workspace, modifying their own strategy.py
  • Mean Reversion Strategy — RSI-based entries/exits on high-vol tech stocks
  • Paper Trading — Connected to Alpaca for live testing
  • Continuous Research — Agents run experiments autonomously

Initial Findings

Best Configuration Found:
  • Strategy: Mean Reversion
  • Watchlist: NVDA, TSLA, META
  • Max Positions: 2
  • Entry RSI: < 25
  • Exit RSI: > 75
  • Best Sharpe: 8.025
⚠️ High Variance Detected

Same parameters can produce Sharpe from -3.3 to +8.0. Need multi-run validation (run each config 10x, average results).

Architecture
